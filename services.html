2. We can build your house in less than half the time of a fully custom home.
3. New construction Expertise.   Read more

Do you design and build custom homes?

In order to provide a high level of design that is cost effective and faster to build, we design and build standard model home designs.

Do I need an architect to work with Zehnlite Construction - Kenya?

No. We will be your architect and provide the architectural and structural drawings necessary to get your home permitted and approved by the local jurisdiction having authority.

How will you keep me informed about the progress of my project?

We will communicate with you regularly throughout the project, providing you with updates on the progress, schedule, and any changes that may arise.

How many changes can I make to your design plans?

That’s the beauty of working with a truly custom homebuilder like us – you can literally make as many changes as you want. Want to add square footage, a three-car garage or move a few walls? No problem – we can do that. The amount of changes you make are limited only by your desires.

Who will be working on my project?

We have a team of experienced and qualified professionals who will be working on your project. We will introduce you to the team members who will be involved in your project before construction begins.

Can I see some testimonials from your past clients?

We are proud of the work we do and the positive feedback we receive from our clients. We would be happy to provide you with testimonials or connect you with past clients who are willing to share their experiences.

How will you clean up after the project is complete?

We will leave your property clean and free of debris upon completion of the project.
